About Me

Greetings! I'm Peter Vanderhook, a tech enthusiast with a lifelong passion for all things digital. My journey into the world of Information Technology started at a young age, fueled by an insatiable curiosity for computers. As a kid, I would eagerly disassemble and reassemble old computers from my dad's office, laying the foundation for my hands-on approach to technology.

As I grew up and built on my technical skills I began modding games I enjoyed playing, adding new blocks to Minecraft with Java and implementing new NPCs/items in Terraria with JSON. The ability to create and experience my additions firsthand in projects I loved further ignited my interest in programming. Upon reaching high school, I took it a step further by crafting my own text-based RPG game 'Dragon Slayer'.

Beyond the screen, I'm equally passionate about the great outdoors, embarking on annual adventures such as portaging the Bowron Lakes or torturing my cardiovascular system with weeklong backpacking trips. I also developed a keen interest in aviation, I quickly found myself flying airplanes (before even learning to drive!) showcasing my love for exploration and adventure both in the digital and physical realms.

I'm eager to begin working alongside professionals on exciting new projects to help expand my knowledge so I can become 'that IT guy who knows everything'.
